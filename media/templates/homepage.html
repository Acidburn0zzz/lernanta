Knight Mozilla News Technology Partnership (MoJo)

Web developers and designers around the world collaborating to invent the future of news.

  • Put your best ideas in front of the people shaping journalism's future.

  • Take your news technology idea from napkin sketch to working prototype, with Mozilla's help.

  • Rub shoulders with people such as Christian Hellerman, Burt Herman, John Resig, and many more.

  • Get flown to Berlin and London for face-to-face prototype-building with the world's leading news app developers.

  • The big prize: spend a year evolving your ideas in the world's most prestigious newsrooms as a paid Knight-Mozilla fellow.

  • Interested? Get Involved About Mojo

Challenge 1: How might we encourage good comments and reduce the partisan approach to discussion?

At the core of dimocracy is open discussion, but a recent trend has developed where discussion on news stories quickly develop into partisan bickering. How do we change that trend and get back to quality discussions?
