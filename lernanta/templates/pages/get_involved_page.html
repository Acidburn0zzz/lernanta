Get Involved

Welcome to the P2PU Community. We are a respectful and dynamic group who are excited by the idea of finding new ways to learn online. There are several different ways you can get involved in the P2PU Community.

Ways to get involved:

  • Create a new Study Group
  • Stay up-to-date with P2PU
  • Join community discussions
  • Join a Study Group
  • Help us improve P2PU
  • P2PU Working Groups
  • Donate to P2PU
  • Join or start a Meetup

  • Create a new Study Group

    P2PU is dedicated to helping motivated individuals design great peer to peer courses. Our course design handbook is a compilation of community wisdom and expert guidance. We also run orientations to help new organizers work in groups. There is also the help desk which is continuously open for new organizers and learners.

    Start today »

  • Stay up-to-date with P2PU

    You can sign up for our announcements list. This is a very low-traffic mailing list, which will keep you up-to-date with important P2PU news, like when new courses are starting.

    Start today »

  • Join community discussions

    The main P2PU discussions happen on the p2pu-community mailing list. It\u2019s a good forum for meeting the P2PU community and ask any questions you may have.

    Start today »

  • Join a Study Group

    You can browse the list of courses and join one which is open, and which you think may be of interest to you.

    Start today »

  • Help us improve P2PU

    Join the developer mailing list, sign up for the bug/task tracker, join the Introduction to Contributing to Lernanta study group, or view the developer documentation on github.

    Start today »

  • P2PU Working Groups

    There are many working groups helping to develop specific elements of the P2PU community and we welcome people willing to participate. For an up-to-date listing of the working groups and the mailing lists they communicate on please check this wiki page.

    Start today »

  • Donate to P2PU

    You can donate to P2PU through our partner, The Mozilla Foundation. All donations go directly to P2PU and help us continue our mission to enable high-quality low-cost education opportunities!

    Start today »

  • Join or start a Meetup

    There are P2PU Meetups happening all over the world, come and join us or organize one of your own.

    Start today »
