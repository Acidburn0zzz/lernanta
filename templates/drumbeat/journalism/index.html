Knight-Mozilla News Technology Partnership (MoJo)

Web developers and designers around the world collaborating to invent the future of news.

  • Put your best ideas in front of the people shaping journalism's future.

  • Take your news technology idea from napkin sketch to working prototype, with Mozilla's help.

  • Rub shoulders with web luminaries and journalists.

  • Earn a trip to Berlin or London for face-to-face prototype-building with the world's leading news app developers.

  • The big prize: spend a year evolving your ideas in the world's most prestigious newsrooms as a paid Knight-Mozilla fellow.

  • Interested? Get Involved About Mojo

Challenge 1: How can new web video tools transform news storytelling?

Video is a central part of many people's daily news experience. But most online video is still stuck in a boring embedded box, like "TV on a web page." How do we make web video more like the web?
